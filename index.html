Got questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to become an Oriflame Brand Partner?▼
Everyone is welcome to join Oriflame as a Brand Partner. People from all backgrounds have been successful with us. We provide a rare opportunity to start your own business, where the only investment needed is your commitment and passion. There are no educational requirements, no experience needed, and no minimum sales targets.
How can I make money with Oriflame?▼
As a Brand Partner you are entitled to order products at a reduced price — saving 23% for yourself. If you decide to sell products to your customers, you can use the recommended catalogue price and earn a 30% profit on every order. If you decide to invite others to join Oriflame who are also buying products, you can also benefit from their purchases too.
How do I get started?▼
As a new Brand Partner you will have me as your coach — guiding and assisting you from day one. I will introduce you to Oriflame and help you with all the necessary information you need in the beginning. You can also choose to purchase a few recommended items like catalogues and samples to show to others or try yourself to become familiar with our product portfolio.
How can I join?▼
It's simple! Fill in the sign-up form above and I will personally contact you within 24 hours to walk you through everything. Alternatively, send me a message on WhatsApp and we can get you registered right away. Registration is completely free.
What is it like being an Oriflame Brand Partner?▼
As a Brand Partner you can choose to save money on beauty products you buy for yourself, earn money on your own sales, or invite others to join your team and earn money on total group sales. Many of our Brand Partners have made a successful full-time career, whilst others sell in their spare time to earn extra income. Whichever you prefer, you are welcome and we will assist you along the way.
How much time do I have to spend?▼
You decide yourself when and how much you want to work. Whether you want to dedicate yourself full time or want to make some extra money during your spare time, it is completely up to you. Being an Oriflame Brand Partner means flexible working hours, so you can invest your time in a way that suits your lifestyle.

Still have questions? I'd love to chat with you directly.
