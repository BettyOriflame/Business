My Story

Hi, I'm Oghenekaro Betty — an independent Oriflame Brand Partner and Authorised Distributor based in Warri, Delta State. My journey with Oriflame started from a simple place: a love for quality beauty products that actually work, and a desire to build something meaningful on my own terms.

Over time, what began as a personal passion for skincare and fragrance grew into something much bigger. I've had the privilege of helping hundreds of customers across Nigeria discover products they love — from the perfect everyday moisturiser to that special-occasion perfume that turns heads.

But more than that, I've built a community. I've mentored women and men who wanted to start their own Oriflame business, watched them gain confidence, earn income, and create the lifestyle they wanted. That's what drives me every day.

Why Oriflame?

Oriflame is a Swedish beauty company with over 55 years of history — sold in 60+ countries with 3 million Brand Partners worldwide. What I love most is the commitment to natural ingredients, sustainable sourcing, and science-backed formulations that actually deliver results.

Every product I recommend is one I believe in. I test them, I use them, and I only share what I would give to my own family. That's the promise I make to every customer who orders through me.
